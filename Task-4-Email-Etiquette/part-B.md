# Social Media Checklist for College Students

### ✅ The Do's
* **Think before posting—make sure your content reflects you positively.
* **Use privacy settings to control who sees your information.
* **Share useful, respectful, and meaningful content.
* **Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.
* **Maintain a professional tone, especially on public platforms.

  ### ❌ The Don'ts
  * **Don’t share personal or sensitive information online.
  * **Don’t engage in arguments or post offensive content.
  * **Don’t click on suspicious links or unknown messages.
  * **Don’t overshare details that could harm your reputation.
  * **Don’t ignore your digital footprint—it stays long-term.
